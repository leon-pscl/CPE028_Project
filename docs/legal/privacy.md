## Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 17, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to RevTech. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly, securely, and transparently.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, disclose, and protect information when you access or use our platform. RevTech is a web-based service designed to assist users in determining whether an electronic device should be repaired or recycled through user-provided information and machine learning-assisted assessment.

By using the platform, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information

When creating an account, we may collect:

- Email address
- Encrypted authentication credentials

### 2.2 Device Assessment Information

To generate repairability and recycling recommendations, we may collect:

- Device brand
- Device model
- Device category
- Device age
- Reported device issues
- Assessment responses submitted through questionnaires
- Device repair history

### 2.3 Uploaded Images

Users may voluntarily upload photographs of their devices for analysis.

Uploaded images may contain:

- Physical condition of the device
- Visible damage
- Device components
- Other information visible within the uploaded image

Users are encouraged to remove or obscure personal information appearing in photographs before submission.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Create and manage user accounts
- Authenticate users
- Generate repair-or-recycle recommendations
- Improve platform functionality
- Enhance system security
- Conduct analytics and research
- Monitor system performance
- Prevent abuse, fraud, or unauthorized activity
- Train, validate, and improve machine learning models

## 4. Use of Uploaded Images for Machine Learning

Uploaded device images may be used to improve the accuracy, reliability, and performance of our machine learning systems.

Images may be:

- Processed by automated algorithms
- Stored within secured databases
- Included in training datasets
- Used for model evaluation and validation
- Analyzed to improve future repairability assessments

Whenever possible, images are processed in a manner that minimizes unnecessary personal information.

By uploading images to the platform, you grant RevTech a non-exclusive right to use such images for system improvement, research, testing, and machine learning development purposes.

Users may request deletion of their uploaded content as described in Section 9.
